About Me

Hi, I'm Sautrik Chattopadhyay — a web developer who loves turning ideas into visually engaging and interactive experiences. I enjoy blending logic, design, and smooth animations to create websites that feel alive and intuitive.

I'm currently learning full-stack development, building my foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, while also exploring backend fundamentals in Node.js. Alongside web technologies, I'm actively learning C++ to strengthen my problem-solving, algorithmic thinking, and core computer science skills.

I believe in learning by building, experimenting, and constantly improving. Whether it's a dynamic game, an animated interface, or a full website, I aim to deliver clean code, meaningful design, and memorable user experience.
